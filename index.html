Sustainability

Berkshire Hathaway Energy is a diversified global holding company that owns a portfolio of locally managed businesses that share a vision for a secure, sustainable energy future. These businesses deliver affordable, reliable and clean energy to customers and communities worldwide. Berkshire Hathaway Energy is headquartered in Des Moines, Iowa, U.S.A.

About

Berkshire Hathaway is a holding company for a multitude of businesses, run by chairman and CEO Warren Buffett. Berkshire Hathaway is headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska and began as a textile manufacturing company in 1839, but it grew to acquire a number of companies in the insurance, railroad, and energy sectors. Berkshire Hathaway is known for its control and leadership by Warren Buffett, who is the company's largest shareholder and CEO.
